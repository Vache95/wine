ARMENIAN TERROIR

Armenian wine is defined by a unique terroir vaguely reminiscent of a handful of wine growing regions in the world. It unites five main aspects: high elevation viticulture, extreme climate, volcanic soil, ancient winemaking tradition and indigenous grape varieties.
